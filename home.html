Escape

You have been hired by El Chapo Guzman to help him escape from prison. Between you and freedom is one simple combination lock. Help him escape or you will pay with your life.

The combination is made up of numbers between 0-9 and you have 7 attempts to guess. As a clue you will be told either Zeta,Sineloa or nothing.

Sineloa's = correct code, correct position.

Zetas = correct code, wrong position.
